# EquipMap Support

EquipMap is a shared real-time map for tracking equipment, gear, and assets across your team. Drop a pin, snap a photo, and everyone sees it instantly.

## Contact

For support, questions, or bug reports, email **ethanaylett@mac.com**.

We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.

## FAQ

**How do I add equipment?**
Tap the + button on the inventory screen, fill in the details, and optionally drop a pin on the map or attach a photo.

**How does team sync work?**
Sign in with Apple, join or create a team, and any equipment you add is shared with everyone on that team in real time.

**How do I delete my account or data?**
Email **ethanaylett@mac.com** with your account email and we'll remove your data within 7 days.

**What permissions does EquipMap need?**
- **Location** — to drop pins at your current position
- **Camera / Photos** — to attach images to equipment entries
